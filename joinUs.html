  1. Provides care with love and respect, prioritizing people’s health and well-being.
  2. Understands the importance of quality and safety outcomes for healthcare providers.
  3. Possesses the necessary checks for working with children and NDIS Clearance.
  4. Shows flexibility and adaptability.
  5. Has up-to-date immunizations and COVID-19 vaccinations.
